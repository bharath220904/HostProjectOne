Welcome to the Memory Card Game!


Match the pairs of cards to win.


How to Play the Game



  1. There are pairs of cards with hidden emojis.
  2. Your goal is to match the pairs by flipping two cards at a time.
  3. If two flipped cards match, they stay face up.
  4. If the cards don't match, they will flip back.
  5. Try to match all the pairs with as few moves as possible!
